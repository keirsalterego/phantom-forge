## Email Sending Best Practices

| Practice | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Send between 8-10am Tue-Thu | Highest open rates |
| Track opens with a tool | Mailtrack, HubSpot, or Yesware |
| Max 3 follow-ups | After that, move on |
| Personalize every email | First line MUST reference them specifically |
| Don't attach resume on first email | Attach only when asked |
| Use a professional signature | Name, GitHub, LinkedIn, site |
| Clean email address | No partyboy2005@gmail.com |
| Bcc when sending batch | Use Bcc for mass outreach |

---

## Common Mistakes That Kill Response Rates

- Generic subject lines ("Application for Software Engineer role")
- No personalization ("I want to work at your company" — what company?)
- Walls of text — keep emails under 150 words
- Multiple asks in one email (refer AND get info AND apply)
- Arguing with the rejection
- Following up within 24 hours (too eager, bad look)
- Sending to the wrong person entirely
