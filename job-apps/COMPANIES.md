## Notes on Recruiter Contacts

Direct recruiter emails are rarely publicly listed. Best paths:
1. **LinkedIn**: Find recruiters by company + "security" + "recruiter"
2. **site:linkedin.com/in**: Search `"[company] security recruiter"`
3. **Lever/Greenhouse**: Most companies use these ATS portals (career pages) — individual recruiter emails are hidden
4. **ATS aliases**: Many use `careers@[company].com` or `jobs@[company].com`
5. **Talent teams**: Some companies have talent communities you can join for early access
